Our Vision: Nourishment Made Simple

Imagine a world where every childcare provider can offer fresh, healthy meals without the burden of cooking, compliance, or complex paperwork.

Growing Meals will prepare and deliver CACFP-compliant meals while handling all the administrative complexity — letting educators focus on what they do best: nurturing young minds.

By leveraging federal reimbursements and community support, we can make quality nutrition accessible to all, regardless of economic status.

How Growing Meals Will Work

1

Fresh Preparation

Our licensed kitchen will prepare scratch-made, kid-friendly meals following USDA guidelines every morning.

2

Daily Delivery

Drivers will deliver temperature controlled meals to partner childcare sites across Asheville.

3

Complete Support

We'll handle all CACFP paperwork, compliance, and reimbursements — up to $5.64 per child daily.

4

Community Impact

Children get nutritious meals, parents save time and money, and educators can focus on education.
